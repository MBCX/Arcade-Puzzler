Pick how you want to organise the table

Standard

In this configuration, you have to organise the numbers from 1 to 15 horizontally from left to right. It's indeed a universal reading style understood by almost everyone.

Vertical

In this configuration, unlike standard, you have to organise the numbers from 1 to 15 vertically. This type of reading style is more common at countries like China, Japan, Korea and Mongolia.

Standard reversed

Here you need to organise the numbers from 1 to 15 horizontally but from right to left. This type of reading style is more common at countries like Saudi Arabia and United Arab Emirates.

Vertical reversed

This configuration is quite similar to vertical, but now you need to organise the numbers vertically from right to left. For those who prefer reading this reading style.
